James J. Hansen

Graduate Student, Stanford Aeronautics and Astronautics

I'm a doctoral candidate in the Laboratory of Fluids in Complex Environments at Stanford, advised by Prof. Matthis Ihme. I also hold graduate research fellowships from NASA and the National Science Foundation. My research focuses on algorithms for estimation and uncertainty quantification.

Before Stanford, I completed an undergraduate degree at Arizona State University and was part of the core team that developed ASU's first liquid rocket engine.
